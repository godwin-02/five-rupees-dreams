About The Author

Fr. Francis Soundararajan is a spiritual care clinician, educator, mentor, and speaker from Tamil Nadu, India. His life journey spans village life, priesthood, education, social outreach, and chaplaincy formation in both India and the United States.


His work focuses on spirituality, emotional growth, human connection, and meaningful living beyond divisions of religion and culture.

About The Book

The Five Rupees Dreams is an inspiring journey of hope, spirituality, resilience, and human transformation. The book reflects the emotional and spiritual experiences that shaped Fr. Francis Soundararajan's life.


It inspires readers to dream beyond limitations and discover purpose beyond circumstances.
